How EventHub Works

Transform your campus event ideas into reality in just a few simple steps

1

Sign Up

Create your free account using your university email (@cuchd.in). Choose to join as a student or club admin.

2

Suggest & Vote

Students propose event ideas and vote for their favorites. The most popular ideas rise to the top of the marketplace.

3

Clubs Review

Club admins review trending ideas, select the best ones, and add event details like venue, date, and time.

4

Event Goes Live

When ready, admins launch the event live. All students get notified and can register to participate.

5

Join & Participate

Students register for events they're interested in. Track your participation and see your impact on campus life.

6

Event Happens!

Attend the event and enjoy! After completion, events move to history where you can see your contributions.
